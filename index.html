Biography

Adrienne Burney is always looking for the next big opportunity to translate life experiences into art. Since, four years old she has always had a gift at drawing. She decided at 16 years old that she wanted to enhance her skills and pursue the Movie industry; specifically big film companies, such as Disney and PIXAR. Adrienne achieved her Associates Degree for Entertainment Technology in December 2017 and a Bachelor's degree for Arts in May 2020. But, in 2021 she decided to transition herself into IT Tech. In 2021 she received a certificate for Software Engineering/ Front-end Web Development.

When asked, what keeps her going and why she chose a male dominate the field, as well as being a minority, she said, "My motto in life is, if you think big, then big things will happen. The only person that can stop you from accomplishing greatness is you. I want to take chances, break every stereotype, have a bright future, and smile knowing that I did this by myself. Because having determination, a great support system, with a strong faith as my foundation will be the ultimate reason why I made it.
